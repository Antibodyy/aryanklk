Graduate course covering statistical methods and data science techniques applied to engineering problems, including experimental design, inference, and predictive modeling.

## Topics Covered

* Probability theory and statistical inference
* Hypothesis testing and confidence intervals
* Regression analysis and model selection
* Design of experiments (DOE)
* Machine learning fundamentals for engineering data
* Uncertainty quantification

## Skills Developed

* Statistical analysis and experimental design
* Data-driven decision making
* Python-based data analysis (NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn)
* Engineering measurement and uncertainty analysis

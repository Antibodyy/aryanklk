Student-led DeCal course run by IEEE Berkeley, providing hands-on instruction in PCB design, layout, and hardware bring-up.

## Topics Covered

* Schematic capture and PCB layout (KiCAD)
* Design for Manufacturability (DFM) for PCBs
* Component selection and datasheet reading
* Signal integrity and noise considerations
* Board fabrication and assembly
* Debugging and bring-up techniques

## Skills Developed

* PCB design and layout in KiCAD
* SMD soldering and board assembly
* Hardware debugging with oscilloscopes and multimeters
* Electronics prototyping and validation

Graduate-level course covering the physics, optics, and engineering of Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) lithography systems — the enabling technology for sub-7nm semiconductor fabrication.

## Topics Covered

* EUV light generation and plasma physics
* Reflective optics and multilayer mirror design
* Photomask and resist technology for EUV
* Aberration and wavefront control
* System-level opto-mechanical engineering challenges
* Semiconductor manufacturing process integration

## Skills Developed

* Understanding of precision opto-mechanical systems
* Knowledge of semiconductor manufacturing processes
* Applied optics and imaging science
* High-vacuum and contamination control principles

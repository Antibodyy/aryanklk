Graduate course covering physics-based modeling and numerical simulation of advanced manufacturing processes, with emphasis on process optimization and design for manufacturability.

## Topics Covered

* Finite element and finite difference methods for manufacturing
* Thermal and mechanical process modeling
* Additive manufacturing process physics
* Material deformation and forming simulations
* Process parameter optimization
* Coupling of thermal, mechanical, and microstructural phenomena

## Skills Developed

* Numerical simulation and FEA for manufacturing
* Process modeling and parameter study design
* Manufacturing physics and materials science
* Computational methods for process optimization
